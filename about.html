Human creativity is a natural, infinitely renewable resource — and it’s coming up with smart, cheap solutions to people’s biggest problems.

INOVA is a newly formed company, that focuses around frugal innovation. As a process frugal innovation discovers new business models, reconfigures value chains, and redesigns products to serve users who face extreme affordability constraints, in a scalable and sustainable manner. It involves either overcoming or tapping institutional voids and resource constraints to create more inclusive markets. Simply, frugal innovation provides functional solutions through few resources for the many who have little means.

Inova wants to bring people with different visions and backgrounds together, to inspire new original ideas and innovative solutions. And therefore organizes innovation camps.
